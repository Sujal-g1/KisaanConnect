Services at KisaanConnect

Soil Monitoring Solutions

A soil monitoring system helps farmers track essential soil parameters like moisture, temperature, pH, and nutrient levels using IoT sensors. This real-time data enables precise decision-making, leading to improved crop yields and reduced resource wastage.

Weather Forecasting

Weather forecasting systems provide timely and localized information on temperature, rainfall, wind, and extreme conditions. With accurate forecasts, farmers can plan planting, irrigation, harvesting, and protect crops from adverse weather, reducing risks and losses.

Water Management

Water management in agriculture involves the efficient use of water resources through methods like drip irrigation, automated irrigation systems, and moisture sensors. These practices help conserve water, reduce costs, and ensure crops receive the right amount of hydration.

Online Marketplace

An online marketplace connects farmers directly with buyers, eliminating middlemen and ensuring fair prices for their produce. It provides a platform to sell crops, equipment, and livestock, helping farmers access broader markets and improve profitability.
